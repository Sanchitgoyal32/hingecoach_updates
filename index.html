FAQ
Is this safe?
Yes. No keyboard access needed. Only uses "display over apps" (like Messenger chat heads) and photo access to read screenshots. All data stays on your phone.
Will Hinge/Bumble/Tinder ban me?
No. MatchCoach doesn't interact with dating apps at all. It reads your screenshots and you manually copy-paste replies. The dating app sees you typing normally.
Is it really free?
Yes! First 5 replies are completely free. After that, watch a quick ad to continue. No subscriptions, no hidden charges.
How is this different from Rizz AI or Keys AI?
MatchCoach is free (they charge $7/week), uses a floating bubble (no app switching), supports Hinglish, and doesn't need keyboard access. Better UX, zero cost.
Works on iPhone?
Not yet. Android only for now. iOS doesn't allow floating bubbles over other apps.
What is Hinglish mode?
Replies in a natural mix of Hindi and English — the way young Indians actually text. Perfect for Indian dating apps. Automatically detected if your phone is set to India.
Does it work for everyone?
Absolutely. Works for all genders. Upload your profile screenshots to personalize replies to your style.
